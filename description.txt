This module provides an accessory that, when worn, will provide the player with an energy shield and bear damage on the player's behalf.
Inspired by the starshields in a 1.3MOD FallenStar 49, the roverdriver in Calamity, and a 1.4.3 MOD Halo Shields
The module has not yet implemented the visual effects of the shield and the sound effects when being attacked.

Source code: https://github.com/fpStupefy/FrogEnergyShield
